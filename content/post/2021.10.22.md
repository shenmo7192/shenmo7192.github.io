第二节写作课

<!--more-->

第二节课今天上，但是今天才正式开始上写作（上节课全都是在做自我介绍）

一节课已经上了一半了，目前来看学的很少，只教了 Simple（简单句），compound（并列句）和Complex（复杂句）怎么分辨

不太知道怎么正确的用中文称呼这三种句子，似乎复杂句就是从句

教材上是这么写的

> A simple sentence has one
> complete thought.
> e.g.
> 
> The boy ran to the store.

> A compound sentence has
> two complete thoughts joined
with a comma and conjunction.
e.g.
The boy ran to the store, and
his sister stayed home.

> A complex sentence has one
compete thought plus a
dependent clause.
e.g.
The boy ran to the store where
he bought milk.

## Cindy's school
Cindy goes to school everyday. She always go to school with her backpack, and she always ride a bike to school. However, she usually forgets to bring her homework, which often makes her teacher mad. 
	
> 非常俗套的剧情

## My Sister

My sister is a 4-year-old girl . She likes playing the piano but not really good at it. When she plays the piano , she often wants me to accompony her, just because I am her brother, and I am glad to have the honor too. 

## Kevin's dog

Kevin has a black dog. He's black from the head to the tail, and always be friendly to people. One day he went away, with out any sign. It was a couple of days later when We all thought we would never see him again ,he came back with a white dog.

后来讲了pron.副词的分类

> A possessive pronoun 
refers to a specific owner:
mine, yours, my, hers,
his, its, your, etc.

> A relative pronoun
connects a phrase to a
noun/ pronoun:
who, which, that, whom,
etc.

> An indefinite pronoun
doesn't refer to anything
specific:
all, any, each, some,
several, etc.
