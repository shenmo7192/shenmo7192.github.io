第二节写作课

<!--more-->

第二节课今天上，但是今天才正式开始上写作（上节课全都是在做自我介绍）

一节课已经上了一半了，目前来看学的很少，只教了 Simple（简单句），compound（并列句）和Complex（复杂句）怎么分辨

不太知道怎么正确的用中文称呼这三种句子，似乎复杂句就是从句

教材上是这么写的

> A simple sentence has one
> complete thought.
> e.g.
> 
> The boy ran to the store.

> A compound sentence has
> two complete thoughts joined
with a comma and conjunction.
e.g.
The boy ran to the store, and
his sister stayed home.

> A complex sentence has one
compete thought plus a
dependent clause.
e.g.
The boy ran to the store where
he bought milk.


现在让写一个小短文，一个simple一个compound一个complex

## My family vocation

My family visited Dalian for vocation. I came to the beach for makings sand castles , but my sister came for the tiny crabs. However, the crabs appeared that makes my sister happy pinched her, which made her cried.
