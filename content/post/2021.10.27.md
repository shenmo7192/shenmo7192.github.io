# 根据听力内容猜意义第一轮

Atmosphere

The air layer that surround the planets like Earth.

Terra forming 

The ways that Earth changes place .

Greenhouse gases

Gases like CO2 that makes temperature on Earth rising, which is also called global warming

Carbon Dioxide

A type of green gases that ......

第一轮听力内容比较简单，而且我们基本知道意思

---

**技巧：**


当听到`which means`时，后面是原文定义

>which means  there isn't any air that suround the planet for us to breathe


定义则是 

>  air that suround the planet for us to breathe
 
 除了 `which means`，还有`refers to`，`that is`，`which is`，	`you mean`，`defining clause (定语从句)`可以作为定位原文给出定义的词
 
 定语从句举例：the man who....is
